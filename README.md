Customer Behavior & Revenue Analysis

## Project Overview
Analyzed customer behavior and revenue trends using SQL and Power BI on the Sakila dataset to identify growth opportunities and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- Power BI

---

## Dataset
- Source: Sakila Database  
- Tables: Customers, Rentals, Payments, Films, Categories, Stores  

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue is widely distributed with minimal dependence on top customers (~1.5%)  
- Sports and Sci-Fi categories generate the highest revenue (~15–20%)  
- Most customers (~60–70%) are occasional buyers, indicating low engagement  
- Both stores contribute almost equally (~50% each) to total revenue  

---

## Recommendations
- Convert occasional customers into frequent buyers through targeted offers  
- Maintain higher inventory for high-demand films  
- Retain high-value customers with personalized promotions  
- Focus marketing on high-performing categories  

---

## Business Impact
- Improved customer targeting and retention strategy  
- Increased revenue opportunities through data-driven insights  
- Better inventory and category management
